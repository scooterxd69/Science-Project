The proposition

Charge while moving.
Use energy with intent.

Static charging asks drivers to stop. Our modular demonstrator explores a different model: a road that identifies a vehicle, wakes a local inductive zone, and transfers energy through a controlled magnetic field.

Research notes

Physics, translated
into motion.

Our prototype turns established electromagnetic principles into a tangible, inspectable experience.

01Electromagnetic induction+

A changing current in the transmitter coil creates a changing magnetic field. When that field passes through a nearby receiver coil, it induces an electromotive force.

02Mutual inductance+

Coupling depends on coil geometry, alignment, separation, and the magnetic pathway. The demonstrator visualises this relationship as a local, activated field.

03Resonant power transfer+

Resonant circuits can improve energy exchange when transmitter and receiver are tuned to a shared operating frequency.

04Dynamic road charging+

Segmented infrastructure limits active power to the immediate vehicle position, supporting safety, efficiency, and scalable control.
