# Research reading list

## Foundational concepts

- Faraday, M. *Experimental Researches in Electricity* — foundational observations on electromagnetic induction.
- Kurs, A. et al. “Wireless Power Transfer via Strongly Coupled Magnetic Resonances.” *Science*, 2007.
- Covic, G. A. & Boys, J. T. “Inductive Power Transfer.” *Proceedings of the IEEE*, 2013.

## Dynamic EV charging context

- SAE International. *J2954: Wireless Power Transfer for Light-Duty Plug-in/Electric Vehicles and Alignment Methodology.*
- International Energy Agency. *Global EV Outlook* — context for charging infrastructure and fleet electrification.

## Prototype interpretation

The AWCR interface represents an educational low-voltage demonstrator. Percentages and wattage shown in the interactive road are illustrative UI telemetry, not laboratory-certified measurements. Actual efficiency varies with coil construction, compensation topology, frequency, alignment, shielding, air gap, load, and control strategy.
